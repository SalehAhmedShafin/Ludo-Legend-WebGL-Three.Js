Game Instructions

  • The game consists of four participants, each representing a color and with four pins.
  • Each player's goal is to take a turn, with each of its four pins, on the board. The one who finishes it first wins.
  • In order to get out of the starting position, the player must take on the die in his turn the number 1 or 6 and thus can move the respective pin.
  • If the movement of a pin by a player results in a house with a pin of the same color, there is duplication of pins, making it easier to move.
  • Upon reaching the last house of the carpet of the respective color, after completing the turn, the pin is automatically calculated as completed.
  • The first player to get all four tokens into the center will win the game.
